Why PageFly
Six capabilities, one knowledge surface.
Every format in, structured knowledge out.

Capture

Ingest PDF, images, Word, voice memos, and plain text. Every format converted to searchable markdown.

Distill

AI classifies documents, scores relevance, tags temporal value, and extracts key claims automatically.

Compile

Generate summaries, concepts, and connections. One canonical page per idea, continuously updated.

Telegram Bot

Chat with your knowledge base. Upload files, ask questions, get AI-synthesized answers on mobile.

REST API

17+ endpoints with multi-token auth. Ingest, query, search, and manage your knowledge programmatically.

Obsidian Compatible

YAML frontmatter, wikilinks, and bidirectional references. Open your wiki directly in Obsidian.

A visual pipeline from raw notes to usable knowledge.
